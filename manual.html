Game Manual

Rules and tips for the combined Pachisi + Puli Meka team game.

Game time: 45–60 minutes Difficulty: Medium to High Players: 2–4 Age: 10+
Setting up
  • Remove everything and set up Pachisi game first.
  • Each player chooses a color and places their four tokens in their home area.
  • Place the dice at the center for turns.
  • Then set up the Puli Meka game.
  • Place three tigers on the board at the marked points.
  • Goats are kept aside and they enter one by one during the game.

How to Play

Pachisi
  • Each player takes turns rolling the dice.
  • To move a token out of home, the player must roll a six.
  • Tokens move along the player’s colored path in a clockwise direction.
  • When you land on a safe square (colored) you must answer which type of artform you are on. If not, you go back to the starting position.
  • If your token lands on a square occupied by another player’s token, that token is captured and sent back to its home.
  • The aim is to get all four tokens safely to the center square.
Puli Meka

Goats

  • They are placed one by one on the empty intersections.
  • After all are placed, they can move one step at a time along the lines.
  • The goats’ goal is to trap the tigers so that they can’t move.

Tigers

  • Move one step at a time.
  • Jump over a goat to capture it if there is an empty space beyond.
  • The tigers’ goal is to catch enough goats to avoid being trapped.

Tips and Tricks

Pachisi
  • Open multiple tokens early.
  • Use safe zones wisely.
  • Capture smartly.
  • Move in balance and plan based on opponents’ positions.
Puli Meka

Goats

  • Focus on teamwork.
  • Block and don’t chase.
  • Fill the center early.
  • Watch every move.
  • Use corners smartly.

Tigers

  • Keep moving.
  • Control the centre.
  • Capture smartly.
  • Work as a pack.
  • Think two moves ahead.
Contribute & suggest
