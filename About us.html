A team-based game to rediscover India’s traditions

This web page is part of a game created by Team Vriddhi at NPS Jayanagar, Bengaluru.

About the game

As AI becomes more pervasive and younger generations drift away from India’s traditions and culture, our game aims to bring those traditions back into the memories of our generation. Through engaging, team-based gameplay we build strategic thinking and cooperation while exploring India’s cultural heritage. The goal of the game is to strengthen teamwork, improve decision‑making and patience, and enhance thinking ability by showing how intelligence, creativity, and mindful play can overcome brute strength.

Learning objectives

  1. Builds strategic thinking and teamwork.
  2. Improves decision‑making and patience.
  3. Reflects Indian culture and traditional wisdom.
  4. Demonstrates that intelligence can overcome strength.
  5. Encourages creative and mindful play.
